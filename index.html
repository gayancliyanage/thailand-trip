🗺️ Phuket vs Krabi
Both are stunning — here's how they compare for families
🏖️

Phuket

More resort options, water parks, bigger beaches. Better for a resort-centric holiday with lots of activities for kids.
  • Direct flights: ~1h 55min, from S$80/pax
  • Andamanda Water Park (kids' paradise)
  • Elephant Sanctuary (ethical, kids love it)
  • More family resort options (pools, kids clubs)
  • Wider restaurant variety
  • Better for a "stay at resort" holiday
  • More developed, slightly pricier
🪸

Krabi

More natural, laid-back, stunning limestone cliffs. Better for adventurous families who want island hopping.
  • Direct flights: ~2h, from S$87/pax
  • 4 Islands Tour (snorkeling, Railay Beach)
  • Emerald Pool & Hot Springs
  • Tiger Cave Temple (1,237 steps!)
  • Fewer crowds, more authentic
  • Better for nature lovers
  • Slightly cheaper overall
🏖️ Option A: Phuket
🪸 Option B: Krabi

💡 Tips & Notes

🕐 March = High Season

March is peak season in Phuket/Krabi — hot, sunny, low rain probability. Perfect weather but prices are higher. Book flights and hotels NOW.

💵 Currency

1 SGD ≈ 25 THB. Thailand is very affordable — street food is 40-80 THB (~S$2-3), restaurant meals 200-400 THB (~S$8-16). Cash preferred at markets and smaller venues.

🏥 With Kids — Safety

Bring basic medicine (Panadol, anti-diarrhea, plasters). Stay hydrated! The sun is strong — reapply sunscreen every 2 hours. Water shoes protect from sea urchins on rocky beaches.

📱 Connectivity

Get a Thai tourist SIM at the airport (AIS, DTAC, or TrueMove H) — about 300 THB (~S$12) for 8 days unlimited data. Or use eSIM (Airalo app) — easier, no physical SIM needed.
